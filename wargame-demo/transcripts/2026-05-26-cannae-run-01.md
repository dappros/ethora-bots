regex). The personas, GM-driven turn rhythm, and historical outcome still came through cleanly —
but the Varro side got fewer in-character moves than the structure intended. Fixable either at
the prompt level (instruct GM to refer to commanders generically in evaluations, reserve `@Name`
for handoffs only) or at the platform level (tighten the mention regex). See JOURNAL.md.
